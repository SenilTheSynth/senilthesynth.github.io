About

Hi!

I may be stupid, because I decided that the best course of action for “person who wants to run a basic bitch blog online that’s not Wordpress and isn’t tumblr” was to… learn how to use a static site generator. Being 11ty, which powers this whole thing. And the elva starting package thing which provided a nice start point. Especially with its baked-in support for the FrontMatter CMS VSCode plugin (which I SUPER appreciate, as a newbie).

Wait, that’s not about you.

Yeah, yeah, I know. I just wanted to get that out of the way. Hi!, I’m Senil! I’m an electrical engineer - allegedly, currently unemployed - who worked around the power industry for a year with my previous employer. While I might not be a great expert on power, I like to think I at least know enough to talk about it a little bit. I also stream stuff on occasion for fun, because Why Not y’know?

Also uh, I’m a furry. A robot? Let’s just go with furry, that’s the easiest. It’s fun pretending I’m a silly lizard robot - aka a synth - online (and in person, once I get around to building a partial). All of the various stickers you see on this website are by Dragonjourney, an artist whomst I love and was my first foray into getting art of my synth.

Why all of this?

Girl I don’t know, I just want to blog about my dumb electronics projects from time to time, and use this space as a way to concentrate my giant “I Have To Post About This” moments, but Twitter/Bluesky/Mastodon/Tumblr just isn’t the right space for it. For the dumb electronics projects, I hope this space will remind me to document my work better, so I can share my progress, lessons learned, techniques, etc. with the world. I believe anyone should be able to build stuff, and so I want others to know what I did if they decide to take that on.

There’s also the fun of, y’know, a blog! There’s just something about having a proper space I can call my own, that doesn’t have to survive whatever Elon Musk or Matt Mullenweg decide to do. Some of the early posts here will be rehashed versions of long tumblr posts, with better sourcing and less ranting - hopefully.
